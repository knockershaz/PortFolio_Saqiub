A Thousand And One Ways To Learn... Which Is Yours?

Hello! I am Monolina. I develop Learning Solutions that empower learners to apply their learning in new contexts. I have a STEM background and seven years of expertise in adult learning. I design engaging face-to-face, blended, micro-, and e-learning in synchronous and asynchronous modalities. My work supports me in enriching a learner-centered curriculum with eLearning authoring tools, graphic design, audio-visual resources, role-play, and simulation games. I ensure that the content is ADA and 508-compliant and represents diverse perspectives. I am passionate about creating courses aligning with instructional design principles, QM Rubrics, and Universal Design for Learning. While keeping up with advancements in online design, I always look forward to complementing learning analytics and contexts with instructional research.
